## Persona 1 — Ana, the Event Coordinator

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Name | Ana Rodrigues |
| Age | 34 |
| Role | Events & Hospitality Coordinator, Ipê Village |
| Device | Desktop/laptop; occasional mobile tablet |
| Tech fluency | Intermediate — comfortable with SaaS tools, not a developer |

**Goals**
- Quickly check in guests at the reception desk without friction
- Know at a glance which guests are in, pending, or denied
- Pull up a guest's profile to verify access type or fix tag errors

**Pain points**
- Slow page loads during peak check-in windows (busy events)
- Unclear visual hierarchy — hard to spot the "Check In" action under stress
- No confirmation that a check-in actually went through

**Usage context:** Standing at a reception desk, often with guests waiting in line. Needs one-tap actions, immediate feedback (toast/badge state), and status that reads from 1m away.
